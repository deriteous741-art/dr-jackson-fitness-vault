Gym • Chest

Flat Barbell Bench Press

Target: Mid Chest, Triceps & Front Delts
Execution: Lie flat, plant feet, grip bar shoulder-width wide. Lower to mid-sternum, drive up.

✓ Form Cue: Retract scapula; maintain arch in lower back.

Gym • Chest

Incline Barbell Bench Press

Target: Clavicular Head (Upper Chest)
Execution: Set bench to 30° angle. Lower bar under control to collarbone level, press vertically.

✓ Form Cue: Keep elbows tucked 45° from torso.

Gym • Chest

Decline Barbell Bench Press

Target: Sternal Head (Lower Chest)
Execution: Hook feet into decline bench. Lower bar controlled to lower sternum, drive upward.

✓ Form Cue: Squeeze chest at peak contraction without locking elbows violently.

Gym • Chest

Incline Dumbbell Press

Target: Upper Chest & Stability
Execution: Bench at 30–45°. Press dumbbells up along a slight arc, bringing them close at top.

✓ Form Cue: Full stretch at the bottom without over-rotating shoulders.

Gym • Chest

Flat Dumbbell Chest Press

Target: Mid Chest Isolation
Execution: Press dumbbells straight up over mid-chest, lowering until elbows drop past bench level.

✓ Form Cue: Keep wrists stacked directly above elbows.

Gym • Chest

Standing Cable Chest Flyes

Target: Pectoral Isolation & Inner Squeeze
Execution: Set pulleys high. Hug arms forward in a wide arc until hands meet at chest level.

✓ Form Cue: Slight bend in elbows; move from the shoulder joint only.

Gym • Arms

Standing Barbell Biceps Curl

Target: Biceps Brachii (Short & Long Heads)
Execution: Underhand grip, pin elbows to ribs. Curl bar toward shoulders, pause, lower slowly.

✓ Form Cue: Do not swing hips or lean back at the top.

Gym • Arms

Alternating Dumbbell Hammer Curl

Target: Brachialis & Forearms (Grip Thickness)
Execution: Neutral grip (palms facing inward). Curl dumbbell straight up toward shoulder.

✓ Form Cue: Keep wrists neutral without curling palm inward.

Gym • Arms

Triceps Cable Rope Pushdown

Target: Lateral & Medial Triceps Heads
Execution: High cable pulley. Extend arms downward, spreading rope handles apart at bottom.

✓ Form Cue: Lock elbows at side; avoid letting shoulders rise.

Gym • Arms

Lying EZ-Bar Skull Crushers

Target: Long Head Triceps
Execution: Flat bench. Lower EZ-bar toward forehead or slightly behind head, extend fully.

✓ Form Cue: Keep upper arms pointed straight up throughout movement.

Gym • Arms

EZ-Bar Preacher Curl

Target: Biceps Peak Isolation
Execution: Arms braced on preacher pad. Lower bar until arms are almost extended, curl upward.

✓ Form Cue: Control negative; avoid snapping elbow at full extension.

Gym • Legs

Barbell Back Squat

Target: Quads, Glutes & Lower Back
Execution: Bar across upper traps. Hinge hips back and bend knees until thighs break parallel.

✓ Form Cue: Drive knees outward inline with toes; keep chest upright.

Gym • Legs

45-Degree Leg Press

Target: Quadriceps & Glute Focus
Execution: Feet shoulder-width on sled. Lower weight until knees reach 90°, press through heels.

✓ Form Cue: Never lock out knees at top; keep lower back pressed against pad.

Gym • Legs

Barbell Romanian Deadlift (RDL)

Target: Hamstrings & Gluteal Tie-In
Execution: Slight knee bend. Push hips back, lowering bar along shins until hamstring stretch.

✓ Form Cue: Movement ends when hips stop traveling backward.

Gym • Legs

Walking Dumbbell Lunges

Target: Quads, Glutes & Unilateral Balance
Execution: Step forward, drop back knee toward floor until front knee reaches 90°. Drive forward into next step.

✓ Form Cue: Keep torso upright; do not let front knee cave inward.

Gym • Legs

Seated Machine Leg Curl

Target: Hamstring Isolation
Execution: Pad over lower ankles. Pull heels under seat, contract hamstrings hard, release under control.

✓ Form Cue: Keep thighs firmly secured beneath hold-down pad.

Gym • Legs

Standing Machine Calf Raise

Target: Gastrocnemius (Calves)
Execution: Balls of feet on platform. Lower heels for full deep stretch, extend ankles high onto toes.

✓ Form Cue: Hold 1-second pause at top peak contraction.

Gym • Back

Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown

Target: Latissimus Dorsi (V-Taper)
Execution: Grip bar wider than shoulders. Pull bar to upper chest, driving elbows down and back.

✓ Form Cue: Avoid swinging torso backward past 15°.

Gym • Back

Bent-Over Barbell Row

Target: Mid Back, Rhomboids & Lats
Execution: Hinge at 45°. Row barbell to belly button, pulling through elbows.

✓ Form Cue: Brace core; maintain flat spine throughout set.

Gym • Shoulders

Standing Overhead Barbell Military Press

Target: Anterior & Lateral Deltoids
Execution: Bar on front rack. Press overhead, locking bar out directly over crown of head.

✓ Form Cue: Squeeze glutes and core to prevent back hyperextension.

Gym • Shoulders

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Target: Lateral Deltoid (Shoulder Width)
Execution: Soft elbows. Raise dumbbells out to sides until parallel with floor.

✓ Form Cue: Lead with elbows, not wrists; avoid shrugging traps.

Home • Chest/Core

3-Second Eccentric Push-Up

Target: Chest, Core & Triceps
Execution: Full plank position. Lower chest over 3 full seconds, explosive drive upward.

✓ Form Cue: Squeeze glutes to keep lower back from sagging.

Home • Legs

Elevated Bulgarian Split Squat

Target: Quadriceps & Glutes
Execution: Rear foot elevated on bench/couch. Drop rear knee down, pressing up through front heel.

✓ Form Cue: Keep front foot flat on ground throughout.

Home • Back/Arms

Bodyweight Pull-Ups

Target: Upper Lats, Rhomboids & Biceps
Execution: Overhand grip. Pull chest to bar, driving elbows down toward ribcage.

✓ Form Cue: Deadhang pause at bottom before initiating next rep.

Home • Arms/Chest

Parallel Bar Dips

Target: Lower Chest & Triceps
Execution: Support body on bars. Lower torso until elbows reach 90°, press back to top.

✓ Form Cue: Lean forward slightly to emphasize chest.

Yoga • Mobility

Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha)

Target: Posterior Chain, Shoulders & Spine
Execution: Hands shoulder-width, press floor away while driving hips up and back.

✓ Form Cue: Bend knees slightly if hamstrings pull lower back into rounding.

Yoga • Balance

Warrior II Pose (Virabhadrasana II)

Target: Hip Openers & Quadriceps Endurance
Execution: Wide stance, front knee bent 90° over ankle, arms extended parallel to floor.

✓ Form Cue: Track front knee outward over second toe.

Yoga • Spine

Cat-Cow Spinal Flow

Target: Thoracic & Lumbar Mobility
Execution: On all fours. Inhale to arch back (Cow), exhale to round spine upward (Cat).

✓ Form Cue: Synchronize movement fluidly with full deep breaths.

Pilates • Core

The Pilates Hundred

Target: Deep Transverse Abdominis
Execution: Lie flat, raise legs to tabletop, curl shoulders up, pump arms vigorously for 100 beats.

✓ Form Cue: Inhale for 5 pumps, exhale for 5 pumps.

Pilates • Core

Classical Pilates Roll-Up

Target: Full Rectus Abdominis & Spinal Articulation
Execution: Lie flat with arms extended overhead. Articulate spine off floor bone-by-bone into forward bend.

✓ Form Cue: Avoid momentum; press heels firmly into mat.

Pilates • Posture

Pilates Swimming Extension

Target: Spinal Erectors, Glutes & Shoulder Blade Stabilizers
Execution: Lie prone on stomach. Lift opposite arm and leg, flutter rapidly while breathing evenly.

✓ Form Cue: Keep gaze down toward mat to maintain long neck.
